Invitation to join local-issues@groups.io

This group is intended as an information hub for alerting progressive/liberal organizations to events and announcements from others on the MA/RI SouthCoast but also groups working on statewide issues and campaigns. It is not intended for discussions, personal communications, planning and coordination of actions, or re-posting online articles you personally find interesting. Events and Announcements only, please. The idea is to share information and show solidarity with other groups without overwhelming anyone with unnecessary emails. By default member posts are not moderated.

Feel free to share this invitation with communications people from other progressive groups you personally know, especially those involved with indigenous, civil, and human rights and social justice issues.
