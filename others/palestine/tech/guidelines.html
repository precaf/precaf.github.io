Group Guidelines

Our groups.io listserv has a page for modifying the Group Guidelines:

https://groups.io/g/gazagroup/editmembernotice?id=139689

The current text is:

Listserv Guidelines

  • restrict your posts to issues concerning SE MA Coalition for a Free Palestine

    • that said, you are not going to get censored for any political opinions expressed

  • keep posts business-like and on-topic

  • ask yourself if your comment will provide any additional information on a topic

  • send a private email to congratulate or thank the author of a letter or post

    • this is annoying for everyone except the one person you're thanking

  • use the WhatsApp Political Discussion group to share your feelings about a news article

  • don't CC: a member of the group

    • otherwise s/he'll get the message twice

  • don't start a new topic by replying to an old one you'll just confuse everybody

  • don't be rude or abusive

We would prefer users exercise self-moderation than to have to start moderating messages.
