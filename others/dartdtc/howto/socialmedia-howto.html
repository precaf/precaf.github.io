Dartmouth Democrats Social Media

The Social Media Committee is currently supporting several social media feeds:

Facebook, Groups.io, Instagram, Linktree, Mastodon, Post.News, Substack, Threads, and X (Twitter).

Substack serves as our website and it captures subscriber email addresses (currently we have about 90), who receive an email each time a new article is posted:

  • https://dartmouthdems.substack.com

We also maintain a repository of public (Google Drive) docs, a Slack feed, a public-facing email address – and everything can be accessed from linktree (though it is not necessary to do so after you know the links).
